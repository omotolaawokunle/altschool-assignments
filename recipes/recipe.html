Authentic Italian Pizza Recipe

You can make an authentic Italian pizza dough, just like the one in the pizzeria. Soft and flavorful, but at the same time light and digestible.

Recipe Information

Prep Time:15 minutes
Leavening Time:4 hours
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Total Servings: 2
Difficulty Level: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 370 g (3 cups) of "00" flour with at least 12% protein
  • 250 ml (1 cup) of water (110¬∞F)
  • 15 g (1 tablespoon) of extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 g (1 teaspoon) of fine salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 g (1 teaspoon) of active dry yeast

Instructions

  1. Prepare the yeast: In a large bowl mix ¬Ω the flour and the yeast. Then add the water a little at a time. Mix thoroughly with a spoon to combine all the ingredients. Let stand for 5-10 minutes until foamy and bubbly.
  2. Make the dough: Now add the oil and mix well. Then add the remaining flour. Add it a little at a time and stir for 4-5 minutes, while continuing to use the spoon. Finally add the salt and mix. Mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough: When the dough no longer sticks to the bowl, place it on your work surface dusted with flour. Knead 1 to 2 minutes with your hands. Then make a smooth, floured ball. The dough should be slightly tacky but not sticky.
  4. First rise: Place the pizza dough back into the bowl. Cover it with plastic wrap. Then place a kitchen towel over it. Let rise at room temperature for 1 hour until doubled in size.
  5. Prepare the sauce: While dough rises, combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl and mix well. Set aside to let flavors meld.
  6. Preheat and shape: Preheat oven to 475¬∞F. Punch down dough and divide into 4 portions. Roll each into 8-inch circles on floured parchment paper.
  7. Add toppings: Spread sauce evenly, leaving a 1-inch border for crust. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  8. Bake to perfection: Transfer pizzas (on parchment) to baking sheets. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Finish and serve: Remove from oven, add fresh basil leaves, drizzle with olive oil, and let cool for 2-3 minutes before slicing.

Chef's Tips & Notes

Perfect crust secret: For an extra crispy bottom, place a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet in the oven while preheating. Slide the pizza directly onto the hot surface.

Make-ahead tip: The dough can be prepared up to 3 days in advance and stored in the refrigerator. Let it come to room temperature before shaping.

Topping variety: Try pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, or Italian sausage for different flavor combinations. Just remember not to overload the pizza!
